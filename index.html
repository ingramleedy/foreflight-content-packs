Source: github.com/ingramleedy/ForeFlightContentPacks

How to install: Open this page in Safari on your iPad, tap a green Download .zip button, then choose Open in ForeFlight. ForeFlight will install the pack. Enable it under More → Content Packs, then toggle the layers on the map.

Related projects

Other pilot tools I've built (or helped build) that pair well with ForeFlight. Not content packs — standalone apps and utilities.

FlightStreamX

iOS app that wirelessly transmits the active ForeFlight (or Garmin Pilot) flight plan to X-Plane over local Wi-Fi. Auto-discovers X-Plane on the network, converts the plan to FMS 1100 format, and loads it into the sim — no manual file transfer. By Flying Dragons. $8.99.

View on App Store →

SkySkew

iOS aviation-weather app that turns Skew-T diagrams into plain-English route insights for turbulence, icing, and winds aloft. AI-assisted interpretation, route-specific atmospheric data, iPad-optimized. By Flying Dragons. 14-day free trial, then $2.99/month or $29.99/year.

View on App Store →

AIRAC Cycle Calendar Generator

Free web tool + API that generates an iCalendar (.ics) of every AIRAC effective date so your Garmin / ForeFlight / Navigraph / Jeppesen database updates never sneak up on you. Optional 7- or 14-day reminder events. Background on the Diamond Aviators forum.
